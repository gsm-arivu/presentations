# How to prepare bid documents?

----

# Use Standard Bidding Documents

## Only three parts need to be edited

1. Additional Instructions to Bidders (AITB)
2. Tender Information Summary (TIS)
3. Technical Specifications

----

# Qualification Requirements (QR)

## Need to check the following

1. Technical Capability 
   - Usually work experience (say 80% of the tender quantity supplied in past three years)
2. Financial Capability 
   - Usually Turnover (say 60% of the cost estimate in previous FY)

----

# Caution

* :small_blue_diamond:

  - QR is subjective
* :white_check_mark:

  - Trade-off between Quality and Competition
* :store:

  - Apply your mind, consult market

----
